# User Management & Roles — Demo Script

**Duration: 2:30**

**Goal:** Show the user admin surface — how admins invite, deactivate, assign roles, and manage permissions at scale.

## Script

1. **Open with the reality.** *"Document governance isn't one person's job — you've got records managers, reviewers, legal, auditors, IT admins. Everyone needs the right level of access, nothing more. Let me show you how that's managed."*

2. **Navigate to Admin → Users.**

3. **Walk the user list.** *"Every user — name, email, role, last login, status (active, pending, disabled). Filter by role, by status, by department."*

4. **Invite a new user.** *"Click Invite. Enter email, pick a role — 'Reviewer' — set an initial category scope if relevant, optionally add to a group. Click Send. They get an email invite with a secure signup link."*

5. **Show the user detail.** *"Click any user. You see their role, their effective permissions, their category grants, their clearance level, their login history, and their activity log."*

6. **Demonstrate role assignment.** *"Change the role from Reviewer to Records Manager. The effective permissions update live — the user sees the new menu items next time they refresh. No restart, no redeploy."*

7. **Show group assignment.** *"Groups — HR team, Legal team, Finance team. Group membership grants category access in bulk. Easier than per-user grants when you have hundreds of staff."*

8. **Deactivate rather than delete.** *"When staff leave, deactivate the account — their activity history is preserved for audit, but they can't log in. Re-activation is one click if they rejoin."*

9. **Bulk operations.** *"CSV import for onboarding large teams. Bulk role changes. Bulk group moves. You don't click one at a time for a thousand users."*

**Key message:** *"Your IT admins manage access the way they expect — roles, groups, SSO, clear audit — without the pain of bespoke permission scripting."*
