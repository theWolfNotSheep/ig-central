# Governance Hub — Import a Pack — Demo Script

**Duration: 2:30**

**Goal:** Show how quickly a pack moves from the Hub into a live tenant — with the admin still in control of what's applied.

## Script

1. **Frame the moment.** *"You've picked your pack, you've reviewed every component. Now let's actually put it to work in your tenant. This is where most platforms either force a full replacement — scary — or a manual copy-paste — slow. We do neither."*

2. **Navigate to Admin → Governance Import** (or click Import from the pack detail page).

3. **Select the pack and version.** *"Pick the pack, pick the version — typically the latest. The import preview loads every component and shows you what's about to happen."*

4. **Walk the preview.** *"For every category, every retention rule, every PII pattern, you see the action: **Add** when it's brand new, **Update** when there's a newer definition, **Skip** when you've already got a local override. Nothing lands silently."*

5. **Demonstrate selective import.** *"Untick anything you don't want. Maybe your organisation already has its own sensitivity model — skip that section. Maybe you want the taxonomy but not the PII patterns. Fully configurable, component by component."*

6. **Hit Import.** *"A few seconds later — and this is the part that lands well with compliance teams — you have a full audit record. Who imported what, from which pack, at which version, on which date. Traceable forever."*

7. **Navigate to Governance** to show the taxonomy, retention, and policies now populated live. *"Your AI pipeline picks this up immediately — no restart, no redeploy. Upload a document right now and it'll be classified against the new framework."*

**Key message:** *"From browsing a pack to classifying against it in under five minutes — with a clean audit trail your regulator can verify."*
