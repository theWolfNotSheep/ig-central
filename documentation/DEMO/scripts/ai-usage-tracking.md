# AI Usage Tracking — Demo Script

**Duration: 2:00**

**Goal:** Show the AI usage dashboard — where every token, every model call, and every pound of LLM spend is visible and attributable.

## Script

1. **Open with the CFO question.** *"The question every CFO asks about AI is 'how much is this costing me?' — and most platforms give you a single monthly bill. We give you live, attributable, per-document spend."*

2. **Navigate to AI → Usage.**

3. **Show the headline cards.** *"Total tokens this month, total cost this month, calls this month, average cost per document. You see the trend line against last month immediately."*

4. **Filter by model.** *"Break it down by Claude Haiku, Sonnet, Opus, or your local Ollama model. Usually eighty percent of your volume sits on the cheapest model and the top twenty percent on the more powerful one — exactly what you want for cost efficiency."*

5. **Filter by block.** *"Every call is tagged with the block that made it — classification prompt, metadata extraction, PII suggestion. You see which prompt is burning tokens and can optimise specifically."*

6. **Show the per-document breakdown.** *"Click any document and you see its LLM cost to the penny — tokens in, tokens out, model, block, timestamp. If a document cost more than expected, you see exactly why."*

7. **Show the budget indicator.** *"Set a monthly budget — say £500 — and the dashboard tracks against it. At 80% you get a warning, at 100% you can configure the pipeline to pause, downshift to a cheaper model, or continue with an audit flag."*

8. **Export for finance.** *"Export as CSV by month, by department, by document type — whatever your finance team wants for chargeback."*

**Key message:** *"No bill shock. You see AI spend live, per document, per model, per prompt — and you can steer it."*
