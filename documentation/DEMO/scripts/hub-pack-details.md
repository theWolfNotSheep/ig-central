# Governance Hub — Pack Detail & Components — Demo Script

**Duration: 3:00**

**Goal:** Walk through what's actually *inside* a governance pack — so buyers can see it's a complete, auditable framework, not a marketing bundle.

## Script

1. **Set the scene.** *"When your compliance lead asks 'what exactly am I importing?' — they need a real answer. Every pack in the Hub is fully transparent. Let me show you one in detail."*

2. **Open a pack page** — `/governance/hub/<pack-slug>`. *"This is the ISO 15489 Generic pack. You'll see the publisher, the licence, the version history, and — critically — every single component before you import anything."*

3. **Tab through the components.**
   - **Taxonomy.** *"The full category tree. You can drill in and see every subcategory the pack brings. Nothing is hidden."*
   - **Sensitivity levels.** *"The classification tiers — their display names, their colours, their descriptions. If your organisation uses Official / Official-Sensitive / Secret, you can see straight away whether the pack matches or needs adjustment."*
   - **Retention schedules.** *"Every retention rule — what's retained, for how long, and what the disposal action is. Your records manager sees this and knows immediately whether the pack aligns with your regulator."*
   - **Metadata schemas.** *"The structured fields the AI will extract for each document type — employee name, invoice number, matter reference, all pre-defined."*
   - **PII patterns.** *"The regex patterns for the jurisdiction — UK NI numbers, NHS numbers, UTR codes, not just generic email and credit cards."*
   - **Policies.** *"The enforcement rules — what happens when a document is marked RESTRICTED, when a retention period expires, when PII is detected."*

4. **Show the versions tab.** *"Every pack is versioned. You can see what changed between 1.2 and 1.3 — a retention period updated, a new category added because the ICO published new guidance. Full changelog, every time."*

5. **Show the traits panel.** *"Traits are the machine-readable metadata attached to each category — business-function tags, compliance-framework tags. These drive smarter routing later — if you ever need to answer 'show me every document that touches UK GDPR', traits make that trivial."*

6. **Call out the import button** but defer. *"We'll cover import in the next video — the point here is that by the time you click Import, you already know exactly what's landing in your tenant."*

**Key message:** *"No black boxes. Your legal team, your records manager, and your auditor can inspect every component of the pack before it touches production."*
