# Governance Hub — Pack Update Observer — Demo Script

**Duration: 2:30**

**Goal:** Show that governance packs stay current automatically — customers are notified when their imported packs have newer versions, without having to hunt for updates.

## Script

1. **Open with the pain point.** *"Regulations move. The ICO publishes new guidance, HMRC changes a retention rule, the EU updates a data-transfer standard. Most IG platforms leave you to find out about those changes on your own — and by then you're already non-compliant. We monitor for you."*

2. **Show the notification badge.** From anywhere in the admin UI, point to the **Updates** indicator next to the governance menu. *"The badge tells you there are pack updates waiting. The observer polls the Hub on a schedule — you don't have to check."*

3. **Click into Governance → Updates** (or the badge itself). *"Every imported pack is listed with its current version and the latest available. In this case, the ISO 15489 pack is at 1.3 in your tenant — 1.4 has just published."*

4. **Open the update detail.** *"You see the changelog — 'Retention period for financial records updated from 6 to 7 years following HMRC guidance November 2025.' You see which of your live components are affected. You see the conflict and diff preview."*

5. **Show the action choices.** *"You can Apply — which pushes the updated components into your tenant. You can Dismiss — which acknowledges the update but leaves your local version alone, with a reason recorded. Or you can Snooze — remind me after your next compliance review."*

6. **Demonstrate Apply.** *"Apply triggers the same preview flow as a fresh import — every change is surfaced, every component is opt-in. Your local overrides are preserved."*

7. **Call out the audit record.** *"Every update notification, every apply, every dismissal is logged. When your auditor asks 'how do you know you're on the latest retention schedule?' — you have the answer."*

**Key message:** *"You stop chasing regulatory change. The platform tells you what moved, what it means for you, and applies it with one click."*
